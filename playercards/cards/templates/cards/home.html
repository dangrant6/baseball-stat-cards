Discover the World of Baseball Stats

BaseballStatCards hosts Player Cards for both Pitchers and Position Players. Each card contains a player's ratings for the past 3 seasons cumulatively. The ratings are based on the player's statistics from FanGraphs. The ratings are calculated using a formula that I created. Ratings can be used for Fantasy purposes.
